About Our Club

Proudly serving the community since 2005

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Gujarat Southern Sports Club began as a small community initiative to promote sports among youth in Surat. What started with a single cricket field has now grown into a premier sports facility in the region.

Our journey has been marked by numerous achievements, including producing state-level athletes and hosting national-level tournaments. We take pride in our role in developing sports talent while fostering community spirit.

Today, we stand as a testament to what passion and dedication can achieve, with over 5,000 members and world-class facilities across multiple sports disciplines.

Our Core Values

Mission

To provide world-class sports facilities and training programs that nurture talent, promote healthy living, and build community through sports.

Vision

To be the premier sports destination in Gujarat, recognized for excellence in athlete development and community engagement.

Values

Excellence, Integrity, Community, Inclusivity, and Sportsmanship guide everything we do at Gujarat Southern Sports Club.
